Costa Cruises is an italian luxury cruise company. They have 9 ships that operate on different routes, introducing their guests to beautiful countries and cultures.

Sketches

Research for the Italian cruise line, Costa Cruises begins. After compiling information about the cruise line, I began to sketch ideas for the logo.

My sketches led me to three potential designs that I polished for the class critique. After the critique, I chose one of the designs from the three for the completed product.
