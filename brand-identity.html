Brand Identity

Okano is a brand identity for a Japanese confectionery. The strategy aimed to establish a brand that embodies the qualities of luxury, serenity, and elegance. The primary audience is women from ages of 30-50 who appreciate high-quality sweets as well as Japanese enthusiasts seeking out a cultural experience.

  • Services: Brand Identity
  • Software: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

Sketching

With the brand strategy finalized, I began sketching out potential logo designs, color palettes, typography, materials, and illustrations to bring the brand to life.
